Our Story

Born from a battle,
built on hope

Brenden Butler knows firsthand what it's like to be a child fighting cancer. As a former adolescent cancer patient, he experienced the isolation, fear, and uncertainty that comes with a diagnosis.

But Brenden also experienced something else: the transformative power of community support. The gifts, visits, and encouragement he received during treatment showed him that even in the darkest moments, people cared.

That experience became his mission. In 2018, Brenden founded the Brenden Butler Family Foundation to ensure that no child battling cancer feels alone — especially during the holidays.
